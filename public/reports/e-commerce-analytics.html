AI
Only 31.8% of orders arrive within 7 days, with the bulk (39.4%) landing in the 8–14 day window. The data shows a stark satisfaction cliff: 22+ day deliveries average just 3.02★ versus 4.39★ for express (1–3 day) orders — a 1.37-star drop directly tied to slow fulfilment. Reducing the 22+ day cohort (12.1%) is the highest-ROI lever for improving the platform's overall review score.
